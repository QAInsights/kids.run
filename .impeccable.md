## Design Context

### Users
Parents who are busy, managing multiple children's schedules, trying to figure out the logistics of pick-ups and drop-offs. They are using this on-the-go or during quick planning sessions.

### Brand Personality
Playful, energetic, vibrant, joyful. The tool should feel like an energetic assistant turning a chaotic chore into a fun game.

### Aesthetic Direction
Chunky, colorful, and tactile. Think of a "toy-like" modern interface (similar to Duolingo or Headspace). Bright and highly saturated colors with tactile, bottom-heavy physical buttons. Supports both light and dark modes, avoiding generic thin borders and generic glassmorphism.

### Design Principles
1. **Chunky and Tangible**: Interactive elements should feel like physical, pressable blocks. Use strong box-shadows on the bottom edge for buttons.
2. **Vibrant and Fun Colors**: Rely heavily on warm yellows, bright blues, and playful pinks instead of generic grays and purples.
3. **Typography with Personality**: Use a round, friendly display font for headers and a highly legible, soft sans-serif for body text.
4. **Bold Contrast**: Avoid washing things out. High contrast borders or solid vibrant backgrounds separated by clear space.
